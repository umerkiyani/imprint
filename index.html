INTRODUCTION

Opened with a small startup, IMPrint was established in 2019 by the students of NED university. IMPrint is a platform that offers you to solve all of your printing problems by the tap of one click. Initially, we started on small projects which were within our university, then we successfully covered other universities' events and moved on a higher scale like covering social occasions like MUNs and made their events momentous. As an organization we believe in customers' satisfaction and peace of mind without any compromise on quality. We assure you to get your products done in a small span of time with a quick delivery on your doorstep. We deal in printing of papers of many type, certificates, brochures, trifolds, magazines, books, notebooks, diaries, ID cards, badges, banners, passbooks, tshirts and much more! For hassleless printing orders in pocket friendly rate, you can contact us
